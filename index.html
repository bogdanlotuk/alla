WHO IS ALBERTA BOOT COMPANY?

We are an authentic premium purveyor of the finest boots you can buy. For over 40 years Alberta Boot Co. has been manufacturing, hand finishing and selling western boots in Calgary AB. We are now embarking on an exciting new journey to take our story and our awesome boots to the world! 

WHAT WILL YOU DO?

Alberta Boot Company is looking for Sewing Machine Operators who will work as part of the production and manufacturing team, to create our custom leather boots and shoes. The key tasks include stitching, repair, and assembly of leather and footwear materials.

 WHAT WILL YOU NEED?

Alberta Boot Company is looking for Sewing machine operators who have experience operating:

  • Single needle machine
  • Double-needle machine
  • Post machine
  • Longarm machine
  • Hand shears
  • Ability to set zippers
  • Willingness to flex operations as production demand requires
  • Basic English, ability to read work orders and understand them.
  • Communication skills, to work well with others.
  • Able to learn new skills and adapt to changing sewing requirements
  • Able to lift 50Lb occasionally (crates of parts or materials)
  • Responsible, reliable.

 WHAT DO WE OFFER?

  • Steady and reliable employment min. 40hrs/week 7:00 am to 3:30 pm
  • Great team to be a part of in a growing company
  • Competitive pay, based on skill level.
  • A safety-first work environment.
